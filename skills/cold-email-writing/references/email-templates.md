# Email Templates

Choose the best-fit template based on the lead's primary signal. Personalise the
[brackets] before use. The cold-email-writing skill re-reads this file fresh on
every run.

---

## Template: Recent Funding

**Best for:** Companies that raised a funding round in the last 6 months.

**Subject:** Congrats on the [round] — quick thought on scaling your sales team

**Body:**
Saw [Company] just closed [round] — congrats.

A lot of [stage] companies use that moment to scale SDR capacity fast and find
the usual playbooks don't quite work at that pace.

[One-sentence value prop relevant to their situation.]

Worth a 20-minute conversation? Happy to share what's worked for a few similar
teams recently.

[Your name]

---

## Template: Scaling Sales Team (hiring signal)

**Best for:** Companies with 3+ open SDR/AE/RevOps roles.

**Subject:** Noticed you're building out the sales team at [Company]

**Body:**
Saw [Company] has [N] sales roles open right now — looks like you're in a
scaling moment.

[One-sentence value prop.]

I've worked with a few teams going through similar growth. Happy to share
what helped them ramp faster if it's useful.

15 minutes this week?

[Your name]

---

## Template: New Product / Market Expansion

**Best for:** Companies that launched a product or entered a new market recently.

**Subject:** [Company]'s [product/market] launch — thought on your sales motion

**Body:**
Saw [Company] just [launched X / expanded to Y] — exciting move.

New markets usually mean figuring out a new sales motion quickly. [One-sentence
value prop tied to that challenge.]

Would love to share a few thoughts if you're open to it — no deck, just a
quick conversation.

[Your name]

---

## Template: Generic Segment (no specific signal)

**Best for:** Fallback when no recent signal is available.

**Subject:** Quick thought for [title] teams at [stage] SaaS companies

**Body:**
I work with [title] teams at [stage] SaaS companies helping them [outcome].

Most of the folks I talk to are dealing with [common pain point for segment].
[One-sentence how you address it.]

Worth a 15-minute call to see if there's a fit?

[Your name]

## Template: Existing Customer Expansion (warm signal)

**Best for:** Leads at companies where you have a relationship but want to expand.

**Subject:** Quick follow-up from [Your Company]

**Body:**
[Name] — following up to see how [product/feature] has been going for the team.

A few customers in [industry] have been getting a lot of value from [X feature]
lately — wanted to make sure you're aware of it in case it's useful.

Happy to walk through it in 15 minutes if you're curious.

[Your name]
