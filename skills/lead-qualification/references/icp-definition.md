# ICP Definition

Edit this file to update what counts as an ideal prospect. The qualification
skill re-reads it fresh on every run, so changes take effect immediately.

## Target segment

B2B SaaS companies actively expanding their sales team.

## Firmographic criteria

| Attribute        | Target range / values                  |
|------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Employee count   | 50 – 500                               |
| Funding stage    | Series A, Series B, Series C           |
| Industries       | SaaS, Cloud software, MarTech, SalesTech |
| HQ geography     | United States, Canada, United Kingdom  |
| Revenue (est.)   | $5M – $100M ARR                        |

## Contact criteria

Primary contact should be a sales leadership decision-maker:
- VP of Sales
- Head of Sales
- Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)
- Director of Sales
- Co-Founder / CEO (for companies < 100 employees)

## Qualification threshold

A lead must score **≥ 60 out of 100** to be marked `qualified: true`.

Borderline zone: 50–59. Flag these to the human for manual review rather than
auto-disqualifying.

## Growth signals that increase priority

- Raised a funding round in the last 6 months → +10 bonus
- Posted 3+ sales/SDR job openings in the last 30 days → +8 bonus
- Recently launched a new product or entered a new market → +5 bonus
