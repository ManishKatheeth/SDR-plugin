# Scoring Rubric

Point weights for lead scoring. Edit this file to tune what signals matter most.
The qualification skill re-reads it fresh on every run.

Max possible score (before bonuses): 100 points.

## Firmographic signals

| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Employee count in target range (50–500) | 20 |
| Employee count adjacent (20–49 or 501–1000) | 10 |
| Funding stage matches target (Series A/B/C) | 20 |
| Seed stage (strong early signal) | 12 |
| Industry matches target list | 15 |
| Adjacent industry (B2B, enterprise software) | 8 |
| HQ in target geography (US/CA/UK) | 10 |
| HQ in secondary geography (EU, AU) | 5 |

## Contact signals

| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Contact holds a target title (VP Sales, CRO, etc.) | 15 |
| Contact holds a close title (Director of Rev Ops, etc.) | 8 |
| Work email available (not personal / catch-all) | 10 |
| LinkedIn profile found + active | 5 |

## Intent / growth signals (bonuses — can push above base max)

| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Funding round in last 6 months | 10 |
| 3+ open sales/SDR roles | 8 |
| New product launch / market expansion in last 90 days | 5 |
| Recently hired a new VP Sales / CRO | 7 |
| Using a competing or complementary tool (tech stack signal) | 5 |

## Penalties

| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| No email found for primary contact | -10 |
| Company is 2+ years post last funding, no growth signals | -5 |
| Contact is individual contributor (no decision-making power) | -8 |
